AR Placement

Scan furniture items and bring them into your virtual room with AR precision.

How AR Placement Works

AR Placement lets you position virtual furniture in your 3D room model for an accurate preview befor arranging.

1. Environment Detection

Scan your room to detect walls, floors, and spatial boundaries using your device camera.

2. Object Placement

Drag and position virtual furniture or decor within the detected space.

3. Scale & Orientation Adjustment

Automatically adjust size, rotation, and perspective to match real-world dimensions for a realistic preview.

How to Scan Your Furniture

Step-by-step Guide

  • Open AR Placement in the app.
  • Scan your room to detect walls, floor , and space.
  • Select and place a virtual item in your 3D room model.
  • Adjust its position, rotation, and scale for a perfect fit.
  • Preview and confirm the placement in real-time.
