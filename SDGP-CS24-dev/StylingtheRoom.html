How Styling the Room Works

Styling the Room lets you customize colors, textures, and decor in your virtual space to visualize your ideal interior design.

1. Room Layout Recognition

Detect walls, floor, and furniture to understand the space for accurate styling.

2. Color & Material Application

Apply paints, wallpapers, textures, and finishes to walls, floors, and furniture.

3. Decor & Accessory Placement

Add virtual decor, lightning, and accesseries to visualize the styled room.

How to Scan Your Furniture

Step-by-step Guide

  • Scan your room to detect walls, floors, and furniture..
  • Chosen colors, textures, and materials to apply to different surfaces.
