Transform your space with AR.

Our app scans your room and creates a realistic 3D model so you can try out furniture, decor, and layouts before buying. Design your dream room with just your phone.

  • Open the app and walk around your space while the app captures walls, floors, and furniture to create a precise 3D model of your room..
  • Simply point your camera at any furniture item you want.The app scans it and instatntly converts it into a high-quality 3D model you can use inside your virtual room.
  • Add the scanned furniture into your 3D room, adjust its position, comapare different layouts, and save your favourite design options.

Once you've arranged your furniture and finalized your design, you can view your room from any angle in full 3D, experiment with different layouts, and even share your design with experts and friends.This helps you make confident decisions before purchasing, saving time and ensuring your room looks exactly how you imagined.
